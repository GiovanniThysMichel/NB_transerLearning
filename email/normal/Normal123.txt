Nonprofit Academic Centers Council 

 

The Nonprofit Academic Centers Council offers two $15,000 David Stevenson Fellowships to acknowledge David Stevenson's many contributions to the development of data and information about the nonprofit sector and the important role he played in teaching and working with undergraduate students. This fellowship will also encourage junior faculty members of color (African American/Black, Latino, Asian/Pacific Islander, American Indian/Alaskan Native) to provide leadership for the field and to assist them in gaining tenure in their respective academic institutions.  The two $15,000 William Diaz Fellowships will be awarded to faculty members of color at any rank, including senior faculty new to philanthropic and nonprofit management studies whose work focuses on philanthropy, nonprofits and diversity.  Deadline: March 30, 2007 -  http://www.naccouncil.org/fellowship_app_proc.asp
